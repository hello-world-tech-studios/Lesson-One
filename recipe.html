Pistachio Pesto

I like making this sauce with my mother a lot. Pistachio Pesto
is perfect for vegetable pastas and other
artisan/italian foods, or you can put it on basically anything else
you wish.

Ingredients

  • PISTACHIOOOOOS
  • Half a bunch of Basil
  • A dash of salt
  • 1/4 cup of feta cheese
  • 1 teaspoon each of lemon + lime juice
  • Extra-Virgin olive oil
  • Half a clove of garlic

Instructions

  1. Chop garlic in food processor.
  2. Add all ingredients save the oil and blend into a puree.
  3. Gradually add in oil until evenly mixed in.
