chococlate chip cookies

They make a good desert and not a snack. They never taste bad. But they are allways awesome to make them.

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda fine salt.
  • 1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter, at room temperture.
  • 3/4 cups packed light brown sugar.
  • 2/3 cup granulated suga.r
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract.
  1. Heat oven to 375%
  2. Mix sugars, butter, vanilla, and egg in a large bowl. Stir in flour, baking sod and salt
  3. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches upart onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  4. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown.
