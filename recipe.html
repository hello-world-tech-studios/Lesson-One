Basil Pesto

Fun Facts

Basil is an herb and is a main ingredient of our pesto.
I recommend having pesto every once in a while because its good, but you shouldn't eat it every day.
Finally, PESTO IS AMAZING

Ingredients
  • 2 cups packed fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Pecorino cheese

    • How To Make The Pesto
    1. Combine the basil, garlic, and pine nuts in a food
      processor and pulse until coarsely chopped. Add 1/2 cup of the
      oil and process until fully incorporated and
      smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
    2. If using immediately, add all the remaining oil and pulse until smooth.
      Transfer the pesto to a large serving bowl
      and mix in the cheese.
    3. If freezing, tranfer to an air-tight container and drizzle remaining oil
      over the top. Freeze up to 3 months. Thaw and stir cheese.
