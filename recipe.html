waffles are a part of history.without them we would not survive.thats why we have waffles.

ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 vegtable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 4 tea spoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

directions

  1. preheat waffle iron
  2. beat all the ingredients together with a hand beater
  3. spray waffle iron with pam
  4. pour waffle batter into waffle iron
  5. wait until golden
  6. grab a fork and stuff your face
